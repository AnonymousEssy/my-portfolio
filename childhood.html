My Childhood 🌸

I was born and raised in Lamu Kenya, surrounded by a loving family that believed in strong values and creativity. My childhood was filled with warmth, exploration, and simple joys that shaped my outlook on life. I loved spending time outdoors playing local games like; Kati,Bano and many others, helping my parents with chores, and spending time with neighbors and cousins. These were moments of fun and bonding that built my character and taught me kindness. I was naturally curious, always asking questions and eager to learn. I enjoyed storytelling, making crafts, and pretending to be a teacher or a nurse my imagination had no limits! Even at a young age, I found joy in creating. Whether it was drawing, folding paper into shapes, or later discovering crochet, I always felt happiest when I was making something with my hands. Those early experiences taught me to be confident, resourceful, and empathetic. They inspired my love for learning and creating and continue to influence the person I am becoming.
