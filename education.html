My Educational Journey

I studied Computer Science at Zetech university, where I was introduced to the exciting world of technology and innovation. Throughout my studies, I developed a solid foundation in core areas such as:

  • Programming: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Java.
  • Problem-Solving: Breaking down complex problems and designing logical solutions.
  • Software Development: Learning how software is built, tested, and deployed in the real world.
  • Systems & Networks: Understanding how computer systems communicate and operate securely.
  • Project Work: Collaborating with classmates to build mini software systems and prototypes.

Beyond technical skills, I also grew in areas like communication, teamwork, research, and adaptability which are just as important in today’s tech-driven world.

My university education prepared me for both technical and creative challenges, and sparked my passion for solving real-life problems through technology. It gave me the confidence to build projects, explore entrepreneurship, and dream big.

I’m proud of how far I’ve come, and I’m excited for what lies ahead. My journey is just beginning.
