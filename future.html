My Future Aspirations

Tech Career Aspirations

I aspire to become a highly skilled software engineer, specializing in web development and artificial intelligence. My dream is to work remotely with diverse international teams, creating solutions that solve real-world problems—especially those affecting communities in Africa.

I’m passionate about technology because it empowers people, connects communities, and opens global opportunities. One of my biggest goals is to build and lead tech projects that support education, entrepreneurship, and digital skills among young people.

Yarns with Essy – Creative Business Vision

Alongside my tech journey, I plan to grow my crochet brand, Yarns with Essy, into a well-known online store. I envision building a creative space where handcrafted fashion meets storytelling — sharing African culture through design.

In the future, I hope to employ and mentor other young creatives, especially women, helping them earn through their art and talents. My dream is to see Yarns with Essy on global platforms like Etsy, Instagram shops, and even fashion exhibitions.

"My long-term goal is to bridge technology and creativity, using both to uplift others and create meaningful impact in the world."
